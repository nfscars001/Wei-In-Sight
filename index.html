Wei Ho a.k.a. Jacky Ho

Watchmaker - University Instructor - Fashion & UI/UX Designer - Textile Engineer

"Wei Ho" is the name my grandpa gave me; it means "Afraid of nothing." he used to be a Fine Art Teacher in school, so I keep using this name for my art to pay tribute to him.

"Wei In Sight" means "All my artworks will stay in everyone's sight."
