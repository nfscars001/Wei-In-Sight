About me

         An artist born and raised in Taiwan, who has been influenced by his American sci-fi movies and TV shows since age 5, his parents always wanted him to become a teacher, lawyer, or doctor, you name it.
         But this kid never followed the rules, sometimes giving his parents and teachers a headache. Years later, he became a fashion designer and teaches at universities and colleges. At least he came with a textile engineer background; he is still an engineer, right? 
         Remember, as did his designs and artworks, he never followed the rules. He likes to combine a lot of different mediums with some science—his painting combined with fabric, coﬀee ground, 3D printing technology…etc. 
Most importantly, most of his artwork comes as a collection with a story behind it, making it more interesting.
